Lagos

Lagos, Nigeria's largest city, is a bustling metropolis on the Atlantic coast. As a major African financial hub, it hosts numerous businesses and multinational corporations. Known for its vibrant culture, diverse population, and dynamic atmosphere, Lagos features a mix of modern and traditional influences. Key areas include the historic Lagos Island, upscale Victoria Island, and Lekki. Renowned for its lively music and arts scene, Lagos embodies resilience and entrepreneurship despite challenges like traffic congestion and infrastructure demands.
